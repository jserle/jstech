Backend solution for ticket broker

The sales managers of a high-volume sports ticket broker were struggling to manage sales operations in an industry where inventory moves quickly and sales occur around the clock. After a careful evaluation of the client needs, an automated system was developed to handle many functions of the sales process, including confirming available inventory, invoicing, updating partners to remove sold inventory, and where applicable, immediate fulfillment via electronic delivery of the sold tickets.

Other tools provided as part of the suite include PDF file management, automated printing of shipping labels, multiple report capabilities, and email alerts.

The development process involved extensive collaboration with in-house staff, including on-site visits to observe the sales and customer service processes in action. The result was a fully integrated system that runs in tandem with the client's existing Point Of Sale product, augmenting it by providing the necessary automation and tooling. As a result of the delivery of this product, the client was able to significantly downsize its sales staff, resulting in improved overall efficiency and higher profits.
