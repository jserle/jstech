Backend Solution for Ticket Broker

The managers of a high-volume sports ticket broker were struggling to effectively handle sales operations in a 24/7 industry.

After a careful evaluation of the client's needs, we developed a comprehensive, automated solution to automate many of the steps in sales processing, including confirming available inventory, invoicing, updating partner sites, and fulfilling orders electronically.

Other tools delivered include PDF file management, shipping label printing, reporting features, and email alerts.

We collaborated extensively with in-house staff, including numerous on-site visits to observe the sales and customer service processes in action. The result was a fully integrated system that works in tandem with the client's existing Point Of Sale product, augmenting it with the necessary automation and tooling.

As a result, the client was able to downsize its sales staff, resulting in enhanced efficiency and increased profits.

Phone Conferencing for Health Care Startup

JS staff was engaged to assist an ambitious startup with improving their web app with more advanced communication tools.

The project focused on building a real-time phone conferencing application that would improve the quality of life for hospital patients by providing weekly, scheduled conference calls together with the patient and all of their supporters, where an inspiring and uplifting talk by one of the patient's favorite speakers would be played.

Major components of the system included:

  • a web-based view where the client could see a real-time view of all the supporters currently listening to their call
  • SMS alerts to ensure supporters were ready for the upcoming call
  • Twilio API integration to construct the phone conference with the patient and supporters
  • Customized E-mail notifications
  • Ability for the patient to "blog by phone"
  • An admin panel for hospital managers to insert branding messages into designated slots within each call

Call Center Optimization for Fortune 500 Health Care Company

JS staff was contracted to build out new modules for a customized vTiger CRM implementation which was used for out-bound phone marketing campaigns from the client's call centers. Used by the over 1500 call center staff, the application also provided a vital issue tracking system used by the client's locations throughout the US, to coordinate problems related to patient medications, billing, and home health appointments.

The application features a dynamic call-push system designed to automatically trigger scheduled, out-bound calls through a browser plugin which sends commands to Avaya telephony software. The call-push mechanism was causing system-wide performance issues due to database architecture problems and lack of proper implementation of SQL transactions. By adjusting the schema, refactoring the queries and transactions, and improving the AJAX-based call-push feature, we were able to realize significant performance improvements.

Also delivered was a dashboard to allow management to guage call center employee productivity through a series of metrics calculated and displayed in real time. This system was used to identify issues and as a result, increase the success of the campaigns and improve customer satisfaction.
